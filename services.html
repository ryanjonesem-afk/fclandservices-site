What We Do

Land Services Detail

Every job is scoped clearly, priced honestly, and completed correctly. That includes working safely by maintaining awareness of on-site conditions, respecting adjoining properties, and following applicable environmental and OSHA requirements.

Driveway Restoration & Gravel Work

We restore rutted, washed-out, or poorly graded driveways for residential and rural clients in Spring Hill, Johnson County, Miami County, and surrounding communities. Work is graded to drain correctly and hold up — not just to look finished at the end of the day.

  • Gravel spreading and leveling
  • Driveway surface regrading
  • Rut and pothole repair
  • Drainage and ditch correction
  • Rock and material placement

Grading & Leveling

Site grading and leveling for drainage improvement, construction prep, and landscape work. Sites are read carefully before any ground is moved — graded to direct water correctly and avoid the drainage failures that improperly executed grading creates.

  • Finish and rough grading
  • Drainage slope correction
  • Construction site prep
  • Yard and landscape leveling
  • Low spot and standing water correction

Rural Property Cleanup & Material Handling

Acreage cleanup, debris removal, material spreading, and general property work for rural landowners across Miami County, Johnson County, and surrounding areas. We handle what needs moving — efficiently and without drama.

  • Acreage mowing and field cleanup
  • Rock and debris removal
  • Material spreading and relocation
  • Hay bale and material moving
  • Fence line cleanup support
  • General site and property cleanup

Brush & Fence Line Clearing

Light land clearing, brush removal, and fence line cleanup for rural lots, overgrown properties, and field edges across Miami County, Johnson County, and surrounding areas. Work is done with awareness of property boundaries and neighboring site conditions.

  • Light land clearing
  • Overgrown fence line cleanup
  • Brush and scrub removal
  • Debris push and pile
  • Pond edge and waterway clearing

Harley Rake & Seedbed Prep

Power raking and soil finish work to prepare a clean, level seedbed before hydroseeding, sod, or turf establishment. Proper prep is the single biggest factor in seeding success — skipping it almost always leads to poor results and rework.

  • Harley rake / power rake finish work
  • Rock and debris removal
  • Soil pulverizing and leveling
  • Pre-hydroseed site prep
  • Turf establishment grading

Stump Grinding & General Site Work

Stump removal, material moving, excavation support, and general site work. If your project involves clearing ground, moving material, or preparing a site for the next phase of work, describe it and we'll tell you if we can help.

  • Stump grinding to grade
  • Material moving and spreading
  • Excavation support
  • Site cleanup and debris removal
  • Contractor and builder site support

Drainage & Culvert Work

Drainage problems compound over time. We extend culverts, install riprap aprons, clean ditches, and address the drainage issues that keep causing erosion and washouts — fixing the cause, not just the symptom.

  • Culvert extensions
  • Riprap outlet aprons
  • Ditch regrading and cleanup
  • Drainage swale work
  • Erosion repair at outlets

General Site Work & Property Improvement

Not every project fits a neat category. If you need material moved, ground prepared, or a site cleared and cleaned up — describe the job. We'll give you an honest answer about whether we're the right fit.

  • Small demolition and shed removal
  • Concrete and debris cleanup
  • Backfill and compaction
  • Contractor and builder support
  • Property prep for sale or development
