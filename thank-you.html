What Happens Next

Here's What to Expect.

A quick walkthrough of the steps from now until we're on-site.

STEP 01

Review & Reply

Ryan personally reviews every request. Most replies go out within one business day — often the same day. If anything in your description needs a quick clarifying question, you'll get a call or text first.

STEP 02

Site Walk or Photos

For most projects we'll either schedule a brief site visit or ask for a few photos and measurements. Either way, the goal is an accurate scope — not a rough guess that changes once work starts.

STEP 03

Honest Estimate

You'll get a specific written estimate — not a vague range. Clear scope, clear price, and a realistic schedule. No pressure to commit on the spot.
